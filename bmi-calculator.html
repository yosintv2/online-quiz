About BMI Calculator Tool

What is the BMI Calculator Tool?

The BMI Calculator Tool is a free online utility that calculates your Body Mass Index based on weight, height, age, and gender. This bmi checker is ideal for individuals monitoring their health, fitness enthusiasts, or anyone interested in understanding their BMI category (Underweight, Normal, Overweight, Obese) with age and gender context.

How to Use This Tool

Select "Metric" or "Imperial" units, enter your weight, height, age, and select your gender, then click "Calculate" to compute your BMI and category. Use the "Copy Result" button to copy the result to your clipboard or "Reset" to clear the form. This online BMI calculator is secure, runs in your browser, and works on all devices for seamless BMI calculation.
