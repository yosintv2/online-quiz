What is Preeti Font?

Preeti is a legacy Nepali font widely used in traditional Nepali desktop publishing and word processing. Unlike Unicode, which is a universal encoding standard, Preeti uses proprietary encoding, making this converter essential for converting Preeti text to Unicode for compatibility with modern systems and applications.
