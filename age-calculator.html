About Age Calculator Tool

What is the Age Calculator Tool?

The Age Calculator Tool is a free online utility that calculates your age in years, months, days, weeks, hours, minutes, and seconds based on your birth date. It also tells you the day of the week you were born and the time until your next birthday. This age calculation tool is perfect for personal use, planning, or curiosity about your precise age.

How to Use This Tool

Enter your birth date using the date picker, then click "Calculate" to see your age in multiple units, the day you were born, and time until your next birthday displayed in a responsive table. Use the "Copy Result" button to copy the results or "Reset" to clear the form. This online age calculator is secure, runs in your browser, and works on all devices for accurate age computation.
