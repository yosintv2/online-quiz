How to Use

Number of Characters (Full-width and Half-width as One)

The number of characters is calculated by counting both full-width and half-width characters as one character. Line breaks are not counted.

Number of Characters (Full-width = 1, Half-width = 0.5)

The number of characters is displayed, counting full-width characters as 1 character and half-width characters as 0.5 characters. Line breaks are not counted.

Number of Characters (Excluding Spaces, Full-width and Half-width as One)

The number of characters is displayed, with both full-width and half-width characters counted as one character, excluding line breaks and spaces (full-width and half-width spaces).

Number of Characters (Excluding Spaces, Full-width = 1, Half-width = 0.5)

The number of characters is displayed, excluding line breaks and spaces (full-width and half-width spaces), with full-width characters counted as 1 character and half-width characters counted as 0.5 characters.

Number of Lines

The number of lines is displayed based on the line breaks in the entered text.
