About QR Code Reader Tool

What is the QR Code Reader Tool?

The QR Code Reader Tool is a free online utility that scans QR codes using your device's camera or decodes them from uploaded images. This QR code scanner is ideal for anyone needing to quickly access URLs, contact details, or other information encoded in QR codes.

How to Use This Tool

Click "Scan" to use your camera to read a QR code or "Upload" to select an image containing a QR code. The decoded content will appear in the result area. Use the "Copy Result" button to copy the decoded text or "Reset" to clear the form. This online QR code reader is secure, runs in your browser, and works on all devices for seamless QR code scanning.
